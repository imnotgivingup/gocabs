About Our App

Go cabs is cab booking software used to connect you to the nearest drivers and take you to your desired destination as fast as possible. Our drivers are always on the move and will respond almost immediately on request to supply customers.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Register with a valid email Address and create a profile. Set up various payment methods and regularly visited addresses.

Search

Enter your desired destination and pickup location, we will search for available cabs and respond ASAP with driver details.

Ride

Wait for the driver at the respective pickup location and hop in the car to jump to your destination.

Review

Once the ride is complete, you can review the driver and support the app!
